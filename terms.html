This notice explains how Yogkshem Technology Private Limited (“Nyave,” “we,” “us”) handles personal information collected through our website at nyave.in (the “Site”), and sets out our ownership and legal terms. We handle personal data responsibly and in accordance with applicable Indian law, including the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the rules and policies framed by the Government of India.

1 Information we collect

  • Information you give us. If you contact us by email at vaibhav@nyave.in or express interest in collaborating, we collect what you choose to share — typically your name, email, and the contents of your message.
  • Technical information. When you visit, our hosting and infrastructure providers automatically log standard technical data (IP address, browser, device, pages visited), and our error-monitoring service records diagnostic information, to keep the Site secure and reliable. The Site may use essential cookies needed for it to function; we do not use it for advertising or third-party ad tracking.

We do not collect sensitive personal data through this Site, and we ask that you not send confidential case documents or litigant information through it.

To respond to your enquiries, evaluate and follow up on collaboration interest, operate and secure the Site, and meet legal obligations. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for advertising.

3 Consent

We process your data on the basis of your consent — for example, when you contact us. You may withdraw consent at any time (see “Your rights”).

4 Sharing and disclosure

We do not share your data except with service providers who help us operate the Site — such as our hosting provider (Render) and our error-monitoring provider (Sentry) — under appropriate confidentiality, or where required by law or valid legal process.

5 Data retention

We keep your information only as long as needed for the purpose it was collected, and then delete it, unless a longer period is legally required.

6 Security

We follow reasonable security practices and procedures to protect personal data. No transmission or storage method is completely secure, but we work to safeguard the limited information this Site collects.

7 Your rights

Under applicable Indian law, you may ask us to access, review, correct, or update the personal information we hold about you, and you may withdraw consent at any time. To do so, or to raise any concern or grievance, email vaibhav@nyave.in.
